Eligibity Criteria for Canada Student Visa

Two type of student visa category

SDS (Study Direct Stream)

Non-SDS (General Student Visa)

Intake: Sept/ Jan / May

For Under Graduate Students (SDS):

12th with minimum 50% above

No gap more than 1 year

IELTS requirement, over all 6.0 no less than 6.0 in each module

For Graduate Students (SDS):

Bachelor Degree with minimum 50% above

Gap no more than 4 years with justifying work experience

IELTS requirement, over all 6.5 no less than 6.0 in each module
