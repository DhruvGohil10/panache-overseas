Architecture

Architecture is described as the art and science of designing structures and buildings. From planning, designing and constructing building and structures to urban planning, development and interior designing, Architecture has grown over the decades to be one of the most integral parts of our system. The construction of any structure that a person dreams of can only be possible due to the existence of architecture.

Architecture has gradually evolved through the decades making us realize the importance of an architect in the construction of a structure especially in the times of the growing population that needs to be accommodated, all over the world.

Biotechnology, Agriculture, Zoology

It is a science-based on biology. Biotechnology has lots of advantages in many areas of this field. It is everywhere and supports us to improve our lives and leads us to a healthy world.

Students who are fascinated by biological sciences and have great problem-solving skills and are proficient with computers should absolutely go for this program. Biotechnology graduates have a number of high-paying job opportunities across the world. The field is ever-evolving and after completing the course, you become a research scientist or Biotech analyst specialists in their respective area.

Dentistry

Dentistry is the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions, disorders, and diseases of the teeth, mouth, gums, and jaw.

The job of the dentists concerns prevention and repair of the infected part or tooth, removing decays, filling cavities, and making models/patterns for dental appliances. The specialists in the Dentist occupation include Oral Surgeons, Periodontists, Orthodontists, Endodontists and Prosthodontists. What is appealing about the career as a dentist is that field of care include not only their patients' teeth and gums but also the muscles of the neck, head, and jaw, salivary glands, the tongue, the nervous system of the neck and head and other areas

Education and Teaching

Education is the method of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, morals, and habits. Educational methods include teaching, storytelling, training, discussion, and directed research.

Education is the study of the process of receiving and giving precise instruction. As a degree, it combination a theoretical grounding in the history, sociology, anthropology, and economics of education, psychology, and human development, and applied for programs on teaching methods.

Engineering

Engineering is the innovative effort of science, mathematical methods, and empirical evidence to the creation, design, construction, and maintenance of structures, machines, materials, devices, and organizations.

The creative application of scientific principles to design or develop the format, machines, apparatus, or manufacturing processes, or works utilizing them individually or in combination.

Finance, Economics And Accounting

There are many career potions of Finance, Accounting, and Economics graduate degrees that one can take.

The study of accounting is all about the movement of money in and out of a business. It covers the measurement, processing and analysis of financial transactions, and how to display and present that information. It’s an ideal choice if you are highly organized, analytical and have a passion for numbers.

Hospitality

Are you always daydreaming about your next big adventure, checking flight prices to your favorite destinations and always researching about new upcoming destinations? If yes, then a hospitality & tourism program might be the perfect career choice

The hospitality industry is all about curating unbelievable experiences for the customers through different mediums such as food, airlines, traveling, hotels etc.

IT (Information Technology)

Information technology is the practice in computers to store or retrieve data and information. IT usually practices within the context of business operations as opposed to personal or entertainment technologies. IT is studied to be a subset of communications and information technology.

If you like working with computers and want to learn more about software and hardware, a career in information technology (IT) could be right for you. IT gives you the chance to design, manage and develop the complex computer-based information structure our world depends on.

Business Management

Business and Management are the regulations devoted to analyzing, organizing, and planning different types of business procedures. And if that sounds really broad, that’s just because these areas or fields cover a lot of ground

These degree courses teach the fundamental skills that are needed to effectively run or manage a business. That’s why you will find Business and Management majors in all industries, in a diversity of different types of jobs.

Nursing

Florence Nightingale, who is regarded as the founder of modern nursing, is remembered as ‘the lady with the lamp’ but along with that she also collected data to prove that the main cause of the fatalities in the Crimean War was not the firing by the enemies but the infections due to lack of sanitation. She was one of the pioneering statisticians.

The nurses of today learn to trust their own first-hand observations to come to important research leads on topics with great significance.

Pharmacy

Pharmacy is the clinical health science that connects medical science with chemistry and it is charged with the discovery, disposal, production, safe and effective use, and control of drugs and medications.

Pharmaceutics is the art and science of preparing and distributing drugs and medicines. Pharmacy's role is to protect the public and give them assurance that they will receive safe and effective care when using pharmacy services.

Sociology, Social Work and Psychology

Sociology degrees concentrate on understanding human interaction, looking at different groups and cultures. Or we can say Sociology is the theoretical part you will require to study and apply as a social worker.

Studying the development of humankind is the same all over the world, although the final product of that said development tends to differ wildly. Still, don’t be afraid to break barriers and borders and go and study for a Bachelor in Sociology in a foreign country as it will convincingly give you fresh perspectives.
